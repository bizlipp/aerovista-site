Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Stratus different from other task apps?
Stratus uses AI to understand the context of your tasks and adapts to your energy levels and location. Instead of just listing tasks, it intelligently presents the right tasks at the right time and place.
How does Energy Sync work?
Energy Sync lets you indicate your current mental and physical energy level. Stratus then filters and prioritizes tasks that match your current state, showing high-focus tasks when you're energized and easier tasks when you're tired.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all your task data. Your information is never sold to third parties, and our AI processing happens on-device whenever possible to maintain privacy.
Can I use Stratus offline?
Yes, Stratus works offline with most features. The app will sync your changes when you're back online, and AI features that require network connectivity will resume automatically.
