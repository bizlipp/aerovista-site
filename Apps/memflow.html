Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does MemFlow support?
MemFlow is available on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android. We also have a web version that works in any modern browser.
How does the local-first approach work with sync?
MemFlow stores your notes locally on your device and only syncs when connected. The Pro plan enables optional sync where your data is encrypted before leaving your device. You can disable sync at any time.
Can I export my notes?
Yes, you can export your notes in Markdown, HTML, PDF, or as a JSON backup. You're never locked in, and all your data remains portable.
Is there a limit to how many notes I can create?
No, there are no artificial limits on the number of notes you can create in any plan. The Free plan stores everything locally, while Pro and Team plans sync all your notes to the cloud.
