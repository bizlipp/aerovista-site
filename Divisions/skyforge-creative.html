Surron Squad

A low-fi, high-vibe turn-based quest game with squad dynamics and questionable upgrades.

Built as a fun internal side project, Surron Squad explores tactical chaos, light comedy, and offbeat progression systems. Players control a wild crew of backwoods DIY misfits trying to save‚Ä¶ something. Maybe the pizza budget. Maybe the lake. Maybe their dignity. Either way, it's poorly planned‚Äîand that's kind of the point.
