Frequently Asked Questions

How do your divisions collaborate with each other?

Our divisions operate both independently and cooperatively. Projects may involve multiple divisions, with each bringing specialized expertise to the table. Our organizational structure encourages cross-pollination of ideas and collaborative problem-solving across traditionally separate domains.

Do you work with external clients and partners?

Yes, all our divisions work with external clients and partners. We maintain a balance between internal projects and collaborative ventures with other organizations. If you're interested in working with us, please reach out through our contact form.

How do you decide which division handles a particular project?

Project assignment is based on required expertise, division specialization, and team availability. Many projects involve multiple divisions collaborating, with each handling aspects that align with their core competencies. Our administrative team helps coordinate cross-division collaboration.

What is the typical size of your divisions?

Division sizes vary based on specialization and current projects. We maintain a flexible network of full-time team members and trusted collaborators, scaling each division as needed while maintaining consistent creative leadership.

Can I commission work from a specific division?

Absolutely. You can request to work with a specific division if you know which one best suits your needs. For projects that might benefit from multiple specialties, we can recommend the optimal mix of talents from across our divisions.

Ready to Collaborate?

Whether you're looking to partner with a specific division or need help figuring out the right mix of expertise for your project, we're here to help turn your vision into reality.
